❓ Help & Guide

📝

Requesting a Pass

Go to "Request Pass", fill in your reason, destination, date and times, then click Submit.

⏳

Approval Process

Your request goes to the admin/warden. You'll get a notification once it's approved or rejected.

📱

QR Code

Approved passes show a QR code. Show this at the gate for verification.

⏱️

Auto Logout

For security, you'll be logged out after 5 minutes of inactivity.

📍

Campus Check-In

Use the Check In / Check Out buttons in the sidebar to mark your campus presence. GPS auto-checkout applies during college hours.

🖨️

Printing Your Pass

Open any approved pass and click the Print button to get a physical copy.

📸 Check-In Photo

Look at the camera and click Capture
